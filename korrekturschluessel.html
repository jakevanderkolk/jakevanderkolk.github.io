a. Proofreading and Word Choice

#IssueDescriptionExampleFurther Reading
a1 Missing umlaut or ess-tset You misspelled this word by not using an umlaut or ess-tset. Compare schon :: already with schön :: beautiful, good Symbol Codes in German
a2 Use gern Remember that to like to do or to love to do [something] uses the adverb gern or sehr gern (respectively). Only use mögen when expressing that you like a noun. Ich mag Ski fahren Ich fahre gern Ski.
a3 Support unclear I don't understand how this statement supports your previous topic sentence. Clarify.
a4 Unclear what you mean I do not understand what you mean here. If I wrote a quotation next to this, this is what your existing language seems to say.
a5 You know a better expression for this You have already learned a much better expression for this. Show me what you have learned by rewriting using the forms you have already learned. You may have to rephrase your idea using simpler language. Ich habe meinen Freund in der Schüle getroffen kennengelernt.
a6 Avoid flowery speech Essays are best written in a straightforward fashion. Focus on stating your case in clear, concise language. I wan to focus on your argument, not your language. Zum Schluss sind die beiden größten Lehren, dass sich die Welt, in der wir leben, um Erwachsene dreht, obwohl die Kinder großmütig und abenteuerlich sein können, und dass dieses Vertrauen auf anderen, wenn auch nicht immer pragmatisch, eine sehr wichtige menschliche Eigenschaft ist.
